Research

Pfeilstorch

Pfeilstorch, founded in 2020, is a currently inactive research community working on problems in the inexact sciences—from philosophy of language to microsociology and institutional theory. We're interested in communication, ecology, detective fiction, gaming, and the rigorizing pipeline from philosophy to science. Our group blog published daily at tis.so, and finished its second season in 2024. An early S1 progress report can be found here.
