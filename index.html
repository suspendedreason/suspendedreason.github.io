Aesthetics

My primary research area at the moment is aesthetic and literary theory. Currently working towards a predictive processing model of aesthetics:
  • Preprint of speculative paper "A Bayesian hermeneutic," cowritten with Thomas Rutten (Research Gate link).
  • "Wait, what? Sense-breaking in contemporary art" (introductory blogpost)
  • Predictive analyses: grammar in Joyce's Ulysses, plot in Dunham's Girls
  • An early attempt at the thesis from 2018.
  • Essays for a proto-predictive framework: on "erotic hermeneutics" in Spielberg & Lynch, foreground/background relationships in Rectify, and the schema in cognitive poetics.
  • Research channel

Software

Fullstack engineer with experience building web apps in Mithril, Node/Express, React-Redux, RoR, Python. Interested in systems architecture with previous study in cybernetics, information theory, and systems thinking from Columbia University & Columbia GSAPP. Into Stewart Brand, Chris Alexander, Claude Shannon.

Two years (2015-2017) as a network technician; currently working as an engineer at Commonwealth Labs. CV available upon request.

Dissect (repo) is a rough-around-the-edges JS webtool that lets you remix The Beatles. One day I'll implement prefigured "track tours" use choreographed setTimeouts to show visitors around the track.
